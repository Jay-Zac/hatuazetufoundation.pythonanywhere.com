About Us

Hatua Zetu Foundation is a feminist led organization founded in 2020 amid Covid to support communities in demystifying myths around Covid, creating awareness on ending violence against women and girls and a focus on the well-being of children with autism and later registered in 2023.

  • Our Mission

    To promote social justice and the rights of young women and girls in all diversities and young people with autism through art, creativity, advocacy, and collective power.

  • Our Vision

    Hatua Zetu Foundation envisions an equitable, just, and safe society where young women, girls, and young people with autism can assert their rights and defend their autonomy, lives, and livelihoods.

  • Our Goal

    To create a safe and inclusive space for girls, young women, and young people with autism, to express their thoughts and opinions on their well-being and matters that affect them the most and be part of the change.

  • Overal

    We strive to build sustainable and equitable communities by challenging oppressive structures through the power of art, advocacy, and innovation, while centering human rights and dignity. By strategically working in partnership with young feminists, activists within our networks, to provide the necessary strategies for uplifting the daily struggles, widening access, and advancing the rights of women, girls, non-binary people, young people with autism, and other marginalized groups.
